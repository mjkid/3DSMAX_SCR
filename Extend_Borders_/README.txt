Version: 1.0

1) Copy the "RacoonScripts" directory to your "Scripts" directory (3dsmax root).

2) Copy "RacoonScripts_Extend_Borders.mcr" to your usermacros folder:
e.g. 
"C:\3ds Max 2012\UI\MacroScripts" for max 2012
"C:\3ds Max 2014\usermacros"  for max 2014

3) (Re)start max.

4) The Macroscript can be found under the Category "RacoonScripts"
